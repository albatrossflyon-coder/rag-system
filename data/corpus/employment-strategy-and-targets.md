## Application Drafting Rules

When drafting cover letters or application responses:

1. Lead with the most relevant project (usually agentic-rust-mcp or Builder Buddy)
2. Always mention MCP if the company is in the AI tooling space
3. Quantify where possible: "$0 infra spend", "4-stage pipeline", "2x/day automation"
4. Keep tone direct — no corporate fluff
5. For Anthropic specifically: reference Claude Code, MCP, and multi-agent orchestration
6. For voice AI companies (ElevenLabs, Vapi, Deepgram): reference voice integration work
